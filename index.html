Abby Tools Suite

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Abby Tools Suite ("the Add-on") is a Google Sheets add-on that helps users perform cross-sheet lookups, compare and highlight data between sheets, and consolidate rows from Google Docs into Google Sheets.

✅ This add-on does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data outside of your own Google account. All processing happens entirely within your Google Workspace session.

2. What Data the Add-on Accesses

The add-on reads and writes only the Google Sheets and Google Docs files that you explicitly select while using the tool. It never accesses files you have not opened or configured through the sidebar.

The add-on accesses the following:

  • Google Sheets data — to read source rows, write lookup results, apply highlighting, and write summary reports, all within files you choose.
  • Google Docs data — to read table rows from a document you specify, for the Doc→Sheet consolidation feature only.
  • Sidebar UI — to display the configuration panel inside Google Sheets.
  • User email (read-only) — used solely to scope saved configuration preferences to your account via PropertiesService.getUserProperties(). The email itself is never read, transmitted, or stored by the add-on.

3. Google API Scopes Used

Scope Why it is needed
auth/spreadsheets Read source data, write lookup results, apply cell highlights, and create summary sheets in Google Sheets files you configure.
auth/documents Read tables from a Google Doc you specify in the Doc→Sheet tool. The add-on only opens documents whose ID you provide.
auth/script.container.ui Display the sidebar configuration panel inside Google Sheets.
auth/userinfo.email Scope user preferences (saved config) to your account so settings persist across sessions. Your email address is not read or stored.

4. Data Storage

The add-on saves your configuration settings (sheet names, column mappings, and toggle preferences) using Google Apps Script's PropertiesService.getUserProperties(). This storage:

  • Is scoped to your Google account only.
  • Is stored within Google's infrastructure — not on any third-party server.
  • Contains no personally identifiable information — only the field names and options you chose in the sidebar.
  • Can be cleared at any time by clicking "↺ Reset to Defaults" in the sidebar.

5. Data Sharing

The add-on does not:

  • Send any data to external servers or third-party services.
  • Use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
  • Share your spreadsheet or document data with anyone, including the developer.
  • Log, monitor, or retain any content from your files.

All computation is performed locally within your Google account's Apps Script execution environment.

6. Third-Party Services

This add-on does not integrate with or transmit data to any third-party service. It operates exclusively through official Google Workspace APIs.

7. Children's Privacy

This add-on is a productivity tool intended for use by adults in professional or organizational settings. It does not knowingly collect data from or target children under the age of 13.

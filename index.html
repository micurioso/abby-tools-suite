Abby Tools Suite

A Google Sheets add-on for cross-sheet lookups, data comparison, and Doc-to-Sheet consolidation — no coding required.

What is Abby Tools Suite?

Abby Tools Suite is a Google Workspace add-on that brings three powerful data productivity tools into Google Sheets through a clean, sidebar-based interface. It is designed for teams that work with large datasets and need reliable, repeatable workflows without writing formulas or scripts.

All tools are configured through a tabbed sidebar. Settings are saved to your account and reloaded automatically each time you open the sidebar. No technical knowledge required.

Features

⚙️

XLOOKUP

Cross-sheet lookup with multi-column keys, external file support, highlight on change, and summary reports.

🖍️

Highlighter

Compare two sheets and color-code rows by matches or unique values. Also detects duplicates within a single sheet.

📄

Doc→Sheet

Pull rows from a Google Doc table into a Sheet. Matches IDs, tags processed rows, and highlights unmatched entries.

Privacy

Abby Tools Suite does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All processing happens entirely within your Google account. No data is sent to any external server.

For full details on data access and storage, please read our Privacy Policy.
